Stamp duty calculator

A tool used to estimate the stamp duty fees associated with property transactions. Users input property details, and the calculator returns the stamp duty amount based on the location and value of the property.

Income annualisation calculator

An Income Annualisation Calculator estimates annual income by projecting earnings over a shorter period, such as a week or month, to show potential yearly income if consistent.

Rent vs Buy calculator

A Rent vs Buy calculator compares the financial impact of renting versus purchasing a property by evaluating costs like rent, home price, mortgage rates, and additional expenses. It helps determine which option is more cost-effective in the long run.

Fortnightly repayment calculator

A Fortnightly Repayment Calculator estimates the payment amounts required every two weeks to repay a loan, helping to calculate the total interest savings compared to monthly payments.

Loan Repayment Calculator

A Loan Repayment Calculator estimates your monthly payments by factoring in the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment term. It provides a clear picture of the total cost over time and helps you plan an effective repayment strategy.

Borrowing Power Calculator

A Borrowing Power Calculator helps estimate the maximum loan amount you can borrow based on your income, expenses, and financial situation. It provides an understanding of your borrowing capacity.

Budget Planner

A Budget Planner helps you organize and track your income, expenses, and savings to manage your finances more effectively. It assists in creating a structured plan to allocate funds wisely, control spending, and meet both financial goals.

Home Loan Offset Calculator

A Home Loan Offset Calculator estimates the savings on interest by linking your savings account to your mortgage. It shows how much interest you can reduce and how quickly you can pay off your loan.

Income Tax Calculator

An Income Tax Calculator helps estimate the amount of tax you'll owe based on your income, deductions, and tax bracket. It provides a quick overview of your expected tax liability for the year.
